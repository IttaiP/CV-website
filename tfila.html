Tfilati

Tfilati is a smart prayer app.
Upon chosing a prayer, the user is presented the current days prayers with all the specific days additions.
Users have the option of listening to audio synchronised with the visible text.

Siddur

The app contains a "smart siddur":
A siddur which presents each day's additions within the siddur automatically.

Rosh hodesh

not rosh hodesh

Audio

Users have the option of davening together with an audio "hazan".
This is esspecially useful for users with trouble reading or seeing.
This allows users who can't read from the siddur to daven without needing to remmember the tfilot.

personalization

The app contains various personalization options
Users can choose text size, audio speed and define shortcuts
Many users, especially users who struggle reading, dont say the full tfila.
Therefore, the user has the option of choosing which tfilot they would like to appear.
Noosach Morocco, Edot Hamizrach, Sfard and Ashkenaz text and audio are included.
Daily times are presented to the user, with the shortcut to the current tfila corresponding with the time of day
